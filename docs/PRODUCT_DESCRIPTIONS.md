## How to Use

1. Go to Admin Panel → Products → Add New Product
2. Copy the relevant description from above
3. Paste into the "Description" field
4. Adjust as needed for your specific product variant
5. Add product images and set pricing
6. Publish the product

---

## Tips for Product Descriptions

- Keep the brand voice consistent (minimal, urban, premium)
- Highlight key features in bullet points
- Include fabric weight (GSM) for authenticity
- Mention styling suggestions
- Use action words: "crafted," "designed," "engineered"
